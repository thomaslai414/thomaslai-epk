About

2026 · Jun · 1st Montréal · Canada

send it off to friends ✧

Thomas writes intimate, personal songs and produces them like theatre.

A Taiwanese-Canadian singer-songwriter based in Montréal, Thomas moves freely between rock, synth and theatrical pop, maximalist by instinct, with his signature choir stacks. His closest sonic neighbour is Rina Sawayama.

When it comes to songwriting, he believes in pressure of form as catalyst for poetry. His songwriting heros are Stephen Schwartz, Lin-Manuel Miranda and Jonathan Larson; His belt-forward vocal style is also influenced by musical theatre, with Aaron Tveit and Jeremy Jordan being his favourite vocalists.

His debut single, “Ferris Wheel Romance,” recorded in Montréal and released April 17, 2026, captures a queer first romance in a 2000s emotional pop-rock palette, written for the kids living it now, and the older listeners who feel they missed it.

Long bio

Thomas Lai is a Taiwanese-Canadian singer-songwriter based in Montréal. His music moves freely between rock, synth and theatrical pop, maximalist by instinct, with his signature grand choir stacks. His closest sonic neighbour is Rina Sawayama; his songwriting heroes are Stephen Schwartz, Lin-Manuel Miranda and Jonathan Larson, and his belt-forward vocal style draws on Aaron Tveit and Jeremy Jordan.

Live, he strips everything back to voice and piano. His debut single “Ferris Wheel Romance,” recorded in Montréal and released April 17, 2026, is a theatrical piece about a queer first romance set in a 2000s emotional pop-rock palette, written for the kids living it now and the older listeners who feel they missed it.
